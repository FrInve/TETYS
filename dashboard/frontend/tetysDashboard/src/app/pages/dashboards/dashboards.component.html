Dashboards

“The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

The 17 SDGs are integrated—they recognize that action in one area will affect outcomes in others, and that development must balance social, economic and environmental sustainability.”

Definition from United Nations Development Programme official website.


For the purpose of designing Tetys, the developers decided to group the Sustainable Development Goals into five macro areas. The reason behind this choice is that by grouping related goals together, it would have been easier to identify broad themes, trends, and relationships, and consequently create different thematic sections of the platform. The team decided to reduce potential human bias and use the ChatGPT tool (https://chatgpt.com/) to obtain the macro areas.

Basic Human Needs and Well-being

This dashboard collects academic research on poverty alleviation, food security, and public health. It is designed to explore studies on access to education, clean water, and health services, offering resources that analyze strategies to improve human well-being.

Economic Development and Employment

This dashboard allows exploration of economic development and labor market research. It includes academic material on industrialization strategies and labor dynamics, supporting understanding of policies for inclusive economic growth.

Environment Sustainability

This dashboard brings together academic publications on renewable energy, urban sustainability, ecosystem conservation, and climate change mitigation. It allows you to browse research focused on biodiversity protection and energy efficiency.

Equality and Social Inclusion

The equality and social inclusion dashboard brings together academic studies on equity policies, women's and minority rights, and inequality reduction. It allows you to analyze research on social justice and empowerment.

Global Partnership and Peace

This dashboard facilitates access to research on international cooperation, legal institutions, and global governance for peace and sustainable development. It provides academic resources on peace efforts and partnerships among nations.
